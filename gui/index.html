Why is this needed?

In Apps Script mode, the proxy decrypts and re-encrypts HTTPS traffic locally. A trusted CA certificate is required to avoid security warnings in your browser.

  • The certificate is local only — it never leaves your machine
  • A new certificate is generated on first run
  • You can regenerate it by deleting the ca/ folder
